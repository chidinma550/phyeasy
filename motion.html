MOTION

Motion involves a change of position of a body with time

TYPES OF MOTION

ROTATIONAL MOTION

This is the motion of a body in a circle about a centre or axis examples

  1. Rotation of the earth about its axis
  2. Rotation of the electric fan blade
  3. The wheel of a moving car

TRANSLATIONAL MOTION

This is the motion of a body from one position in space to another without rotating. examples are;

  1. Book sliding from one end of an inclined table to another
  2. An athlete involed in a race
  3. A car travelling from one state to another

RANDOM MOTION

This is the motion of the body in an irregular or haphazardly manner without a predict manner. examples are;

  1. Motion of a flying butterfly
  2. Motion of smoke particles suspended in air
  3. Brownian motion;This is the irregular motion of particles of various kind suspended in water
